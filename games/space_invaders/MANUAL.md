# Space Invaders - Game Manual

## Overview
A classic arcade shooter where you must defend Earth from waves of descending aliens.

## How to Play
*   **Objective**: Destroy all aliens before they reach the bottom of the screen.
*   **Lives**: You start with 3 lives. Avoid enemy fire and collisions.

## Controls
*   **Arrow Left / Right**: Move your ship.
*   **Space**: Fire laser.
*   **Touch/Mouse**: Tap left/right side of screen to move, tap to shoot.

## Mechanics
*   **Aliens**: Move in a grid pattern, dropping down when they hit the screen edge.
*   **Speed**: Aliens speed up as you destroy them.
*   **Scoring**: destroying aliens grants points.
