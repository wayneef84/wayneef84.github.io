# Breakout - Game Manual

## Overview
Destroy all bricks by bouncing a ball off your paddle.

## How to Play
*   **Objective**: Clear the screen of bricks.
*   **Lives**: Don't let the ball fall below the paddle.

## Controls
*   **Arrow Left / Right**: Move paddle.
*   **Mouse / Touch**: Drag to move paddle.

## Mechanics
*   **Power-ups**: (None currently implemented, standard gameplay).
*   **Scoring**: Points awarded for each brick destroyed. Pitch increases with combo.
