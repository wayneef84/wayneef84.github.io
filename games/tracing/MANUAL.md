# Tracing Suite - User Manual

## Overview
An educational tool designed to help users learn to write letters, words, and sentences.

## Modes
1.  **Letters**: Practice individual alphabet characters.
2.  **Words**: Combine letters to form words with visual cues.
3.  **Sentences**: Practice full sentences.
4.  **Chinese**: Practice Chinese characters (Stroke order).

## How to Play
*   **Select Pack**: Choose a category (e.g., Uppercase, Lowercase, Animals).
*   **Trace**: Follow the guided lines.
    *   **Ghost Mode**: Follow the moving dot.
    *   **Free Mode**: Trace at your own pace.
*   **Audio**: Listen to the letter names and words (uses Text-to-Speech).

## Controls
*   **Mouse / Touch**: Draw on the canvas.
