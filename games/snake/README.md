# F.O.N.G. Neon Snake

## What is this?
F.O.N.G. Neon Snake is a modern, glow-aesthetic arcade game that updates the classic snake mechanics with fluid controls and visual flair.

**Who should play:** Anyone looking for a quick, reflex-testing game that respects the classic formula while looking beautiful.
**Why it exists:** To demonstrate high-performance canvas rendering with glow effects and responsive touch controls.

---

## Quick Start (30 seconds)
1. Launch the game from the F.O.N.G. Master Hub.
2. Swipe (on mobile) or use Arrow Keys (on desktop) to change the snake's direction.
3. Eat the glowing food to grow longer and increase your score.
4. Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail!

**Pro Tip:** Quick turns are buffered, allowing you to queue up your next move before you reach an intersection!

---

## Features
- ✨ **Neon Aesthetics** - Vibrant, glowing visuals powered by canvas shadow effects.
- 🎮 **Input Buffering** - Advanced input handling ensures your turns are never missed, even at high speeds.
- 📱 **Omni-Directional Swipes** - Play comfortably with one hand using intuitive swipe gestures anywhere on the screen.
- ⚡ **Dynamic Speed** - The game gradually increases in speed as your snake grows, ramping up the challenge.

---

## How to Play

**Objective:**
Grow as long as possible by consuming food without crashing.

**Rules:**
- Eating food adds length to your snake and points to your score.
- Colliding with the wall ends the game immediately.
- Colliding with your own body ends the game immediately.

**Controls:**
- **Mobile:** Swipe Up, Down, Left, or Right anywhere on the screen.
- **Desktop:** Arrow Keys or WASD.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
**Issue:** The game lags on older devices.
- **Solution:** The neon glow effect (`shadowBlur`) can be performance-intensive. Check settings to disable high-quality effects if available.

### Known Limitations
- **Multiplayer:** Single player only.
