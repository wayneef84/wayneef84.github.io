# 5 Card Draw - Game Manual

## Overview
Classic Poker variant.

## How to Play
1.  **Ante**: Place initial bet.
2.  **Deal**: Receive 5 cards.
3.  **Draw Phase**: Select cards to discard and replace.
4.  **Showdown**: Best hand wins the pot.

## Hand Rankings
1.  Royal Flush
2.  Straight Flush
3.  Four of a Kind
4.  Full House
5.  Flush
6.  Straight
7.  Three of a Kind
8.  Two Pair
9.  Pair
10. High Card
