# War - Game Manual

## Overview
The classic card game of chance.

## How to Play
*   **Objective**: Win all the cards.
*   **Gameplay**: Both players flip the top card of their deck. Higher card wins both.
*   **War**: If cards match, a "War" is initiated. Additional cards are staked, and a new flip determines the winner of the pile.

## Modes
*   **Endless**: Cards are recycled from the graveyard.
*   **Standard**: Game ends when a deck is empty.

## Controls
*   **Flip**: Click the deck or "Deal" button.
*   **Auto-Play**: Toggle available in settings.
