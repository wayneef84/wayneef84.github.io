# Blackjack - Game Manual

## Overview
Classic Casino Blackjack (21). Beat the dealer without going over 21.

## How to Play
1.  **Place Bet**: Select chip amount and place bet.
2.  **Deal**: Receive 2 cards. Dealer receives 1 face up, 1 face down.
3.  **Actions**:
    *   **Hit**: Take another card.
    *   **Stand**: End your turn.
    *   **Double Down**: Double bet, take exactly one more card (First turn only).
4.  **Win Condition**: Have a higher total than the dealer without busting (>21).

## Rules
*   **Blackjack**: Ace + 10/Face card pays 3:2.
*   **Dealer**: Hits on 16, Stands on 17.
*   **Bust**: If you exceed 21, you lose immediately.
