# Pong - Game Manual

## Overview
The arcade classic. Play against the CPU in a test of reflexes.

## How to Play
*   **Objective**: Score points by getting the ball past the opponent's paddle.
*   **Winning**: First to score keeps playing! (Endless).

## Controls
*   **Arrow Up / Down**: Move paddle.
*   **W / S**: Alternative keys.
*   **Mouse / Touch**: Paddle follows vertical position.

## Opponent
*   The CPU tracks the ball but has a limited speed, allowing you to win with sharp angles.
